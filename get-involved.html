Take Part in Washington University Campus Activities!

Being involved on campus is a crucial aspect of staying happy and healthy during your time here at Washington University. Studies have shown that students that are more socially engaged (in moderation) tend to do better on their tests and have a generally more positive disposition. So what are yo waiting for?? Get involved!

What does being involved look like?

Whether it's meeting with a campus club, painting the underpass, or studying with friends in Whisper's Cafe, Washington University students know that staying active on campus and being able to balance work with play is key to succeeding for the next four years. Take it from some of your peers!

Testemonials

Markus Phillips

I can truley say it's the best coping method I have for dealing with stress! The people in the club are wonderful, it's so nice to be around people with common interests, even if it is something as simple as knitting!"
